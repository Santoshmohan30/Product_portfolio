How I Work

Problem Identification

I start by clarifying the operational or user problem, where the friction is happening, and why it matters to the business.

User Understanding

I map the workflow, understand stakeholder needs, and identify the highest-friction tasks that deserve product attention first.

Product Strategy

I define the product goal, choose the right scope, and align priorities with measurable outcomes instead of feature volume.

Execution with Engineering Teams

I translate goals into clear tasks, collaborate closely with engineers, and keep delivery moving through agile planning.

Measurement through Metrics

I track adoption, efficiency, reliability, and workflow impact to determine whether the product is solving the intended problem.

Iteration and Improvement

I use results and feedback to refine the roadmap, remove bottlenecks, and improve the next version of the product.
